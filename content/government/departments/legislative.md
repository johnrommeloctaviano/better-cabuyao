# Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Cabuyao (City Council)

The **Sangguniang Panlungsod ng Cabuyao** is the legislative body of Cabuyao City. It is responsible for enacting city ordinances, approving the annual budget, and overseeing local governance in accordance with the **Local Government Code of 1991** and the **Cabuyao City Charter (Republic Act No. 10163)**.

## Powers and Functions

### Core Legislative Powers

- Enact city ordinances (local laws)
- Approve the Annual Appropriation Ordinance (city budget)
- Approve city development plans and programs
- Regulate businesses, land use, traffic, and public safety
- Generate city revenue through tax ordinances

### Additional Powers

- Override the Mayor's veto by a two-thirds (2/3) vote
- Approve appointments of key department heads
- Create or abolish city offices
- Conduct legislative investigations and public hearings
- Issue franchises (utilities, markets, transport)

### Policy Areas

Health, Education, Environment, Housing, Transportation, Social Welfare, Public Safety, Trade and Industry, Housing and Urban Planning

## How a Law Is Made in Cabuyao City

1. **Proposal** — An ordinance or resolution is filed by a councilor, the Mayor, or a committee.
2. **Committee Review** — Assigned to the relevant committee; public hearings may be conducted.
3. **Session Deliberation** — Discussed and debated in regular session.
4. **Voting** — Majority vote required for passage.
5. **Mayor's Action** — Mayor signs (approves) or vetoes the measure.
6. **Override (if vetoed)** — A two-thirds vote of the council overrides the veto and enacts the measure into law.

## Term and Election Details

- Councilors are elected **city-wide (at large)** every three years
- **Current term:** 2025–2028
- Maximum of **three consecutive terms** (9 years)
