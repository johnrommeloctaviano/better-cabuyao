# Cabuyao City: Renew Business Permits and Pay Local Business Taxes

Existing businesses in Cabuyao must renew their **Mayor's Permit** and pay **local business taxes** annually, typically in January.

---

## Annual Business Permit Renewal

**Where:** Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO), Cabuyao City Hall

**When:** January 1–20 of each year (renew before the deadline to avoid surcharges)

**Requirements (typical)**

- Accomplished renewal application form
- Previous year's business permit (original)
- Latest official receipts of tax payments
- Updated Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (if required)
- Updated Sanitary Permit (if required)
- Barangay Clearance (current year)
- Financial statements or gross sales declaration

**Cost:** Computed based on gross sales / receipts from the previous year.

> Renewing after January 20 incurs **surcharges and penalties**. File early.

---

## Local Business Taxes

Local business taxes are paid at the **City Treasurer's Office**, Cabuyao City Hall.

- Tax is based on **gross annual sales or receipts** from the preceding year
- Paid **quarterly or annually** (annual payment due in January)
- Businesses may pay in four equal installments

---

## Real Property Tax (if applicable)

If your business owns real property in Cabuyao:

- Real Property Tax (RPT) is due **January 31** for discounts; full payment by **March 31**
- Pay at the City Treasurer's Office

---

## Online / Cashless Payment

Check with the **City Treasurer's Office** for current options for cashless payment of taxes and fees.

- Connecting with a BIR-registered tax professional
- Renewal process guidance for first-timers
