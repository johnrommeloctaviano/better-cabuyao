# Cabuyao City: Join Trade Fairs, Business Expos, and Market Access Programs

Cabuyao City businesses can expand their market reach through trade fairs, national retail programs, and DTI market access initiatives.

---

## 1. Local Trade Fairs and Expos

The LGU and DTI regularly organize trade fairs and business expos where Cabuyao MSMEs can:

- Showcase and sell their products directly to buyers
- Connect with suppliers, distributors, and investors
- Access exposure to regional and national markets

**Example:** 2026 Agri-Tourism Trade Fair in Cabuyao

**How to join**

1. Monitor announcements from the **Cabuyao City LGU Facebook page**, **Negosyo Center Cabuyao**, and **DTI Region IV-A**.
2. Register your business / product with the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao**.
3. Submit required documents (business permit, DTI registration, product samples/photos as required).
4. Attend briefings and prepare your booth or display.

---

## 2. OTOP (One Town One Product)

The **OTOP Philippines** program promotes locally distinctive Cabuyao products in national and international markets.

**Benefits**

- Branding and packaging support from DTI
- Inclusion in national OTOP directories and marketplaces
- Trade fair participation support
- Opportunity to sell in Go Lokal! stores

**How to join:** Register your product at the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** or **DTI Region IV-A CALABARZON**.

---

## 3. Go Lokal! Program

**Go Lokal!** enables MSMEs to sell their products in **DTI-partnered locations** including malls and airports nationwide.

**Ideal for:** Businesses with packaged, retail-ready products (food, crafts, local goods)

**How to join:** Apply through the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** — they will assess your product and connect you with the program coordinator.

---

## 4. Online Selling and E-Commerce

DTI and the Negosyo Center offer training to help Cabuyao businesses reach online buyers:

| Platform / Training                    | What You Learn                                       |
| -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Shopee / Lazada / TikTok Shop          | Setting up an online store, product listing, pricing |
| Facebook Shop                          | Social selling, ads basics                           |
| SME Roving Academy (Digital Marketing) | Online marketing, e-commerce strategy                |
| Logistics and fulfillment              | Packaging, shipping, rider services                  |

**How to join training:** Inquire at the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** for the current training schedule — most sessions are **free**.

---

## 5. Tourism Promotions

The LGU coordinates with the tourism sector for events and promotions that include local food, crafts, and products. Contact the **City Tourism Office** for participation opportunities.

---

## Key Contacts

- **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** — trade fair registration, OTOP, Go Lokal!, online selling training
- **DTI Region IV-A (CALABARZON)** — national programs, OTOP, market linkage
- **City Tourism Office, Cabuyao City Hall** — tourism events and promotions
