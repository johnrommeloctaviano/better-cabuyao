# Cabuyao City: Rent Stalls in Public Markets

Public market stalls in Cabuyao City are managed by the **City Government of Cabuyao** through the relevant market administration office.

---

## How to Apply for a Market Stall

1. **Visit Cabuyao City Hall** — go to the **Market Administration Office** or the **City Administrator's Office** to inquire about available stalls.
2. **Ask about current vacancies** — stall availability changes. Request the list of available slots and their locations within the market.
3. **Submit requirements** (typical):
   - Accomplished application form
   - Valid ID
   - Barangay Certificate of Residency
   - Business Permit (or proof of application)
   - 2x2 ID photos
4. **Attend evaluation or lottery** — stall assignment may follow a queuing or lottery system depending on demand.
5. **Sign the lease agreement** and pay the initial fees.

---

## Stall Rental Fees

Fees vary depending on:

- Stall location (section and floor)
- Stall size
- Type of goods sold

Confirm current rates at the **Market Administration Office, Cabuyao City Hall**.

---

## Rules and Obligations

- Pay monthly rental fees on time at the City Treasurer's Office
- Maintain cleanliness of your stall and surrounding area
- Follow market rules on hours of operation, prohibited goods, and health standards
- Renew your lease annually along with your business permit

---

## Negosyo Center Tip

If you are new to market vending, the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** can help you:

- Register your business name (DTI)
- Apply for a market vendor's permit
- Access livelihood starter kits (for qualifying vendors)

---

## Contact

- **Market Administration Office** — Cabuyao City Hall
- **City Treasurer's Office** — for rental payments
- **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** — MSME and vendor support
