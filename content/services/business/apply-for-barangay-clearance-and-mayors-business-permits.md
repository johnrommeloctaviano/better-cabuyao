# Cabuyao City: Apply for Barangay Clearance and Mayor's Business Permit

Starting a business in Cabuyao requires several permits and registrations. This guide walks you through the complete process — from business name registration to your Mayor's Permit.

---

## Complete Business Registration Flow

| Step | What                                              | Where                                                           |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Register your business name                       | DTI (sole proprietorship) or SEC (corporation/partnership)      |
| 2    | Get Barangay Clearance                            | Your barangay hall                                              |
| 3    | Apply for Mayor's Permit (Business Permit)        | Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO), Cabuyao City Hall |
| 4    | Secure sanitary permit and fire safety inspection | City Health Office / BFP                                        |
| 5    | Get Zoning Clearance                              | City Planning and Development Office                            |
| 6    | Register with BIR                                 | Revenue District Office                                         |
| 7    | Register with SSS / PhilHealth / Pag-IBIG         | (if hiring employees)                                           |

---

## Step 1: Business Name Registration (DTI)

For sole proprietorships, register your business name with the **Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)**.

**Where to get help:** Negosyo Center Cabuyao offers **free assistance** with DTI business name registration.

---

## Step 2: Barangay Clearance

**Where:** Your barangay hall (barangay where the business will operate)

**Requirements (typical)**

- Valid ID
- Proof of business address (lease contract or property title)
- DTI Certificate of Business Name Registration

**Cost:** Barangay-level fee (varies)

---

## Step 3: Mayor's Permit (New Business)

**Where:** Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO), Cabuyao City Hall

**Requirements (typical for new business)**

- Accomplished application form (available at BPLO)
- DTI / SEC / CDA registration certificate
- Barangay Clearance
- Zoning Clearance
- Sanitary Permit (from City Health Office)
- Fire Safety Inspection Certificate (from BFP)
- Lease contract or property ownership documents
- Valid ID of applicant / authorized representative

**Cost:** Varies based on business type, capitalization, and location.

---

## Negosyo Center Cabuyao — One-Stop Help

The **Negosyo Center Cabuyao** (DTI-LGU partnership) provides free guidance for every step:

- Business name registration
- BMBE (Barangay Micro Business Enterprise) certification assistance
- Permit guidance and follow-through
- Tax and regulatory basics (BIR, SEC)

**Go here first** if you are starting a new business — they will walk you through the entire process.

---

## BMBE Registration (for Micro Businesses)

If your business has total assets of ₱3 million or below (excluding land):

- Register as a **Barangay Micro Business Enterprise (BMBE)**
- Benefits: **Income tax exemption**, access to government financing, simplified compliance

Apply at the **BPLO, Cabuyao City Hall** or through the **Negosyo Center Cabuyao**.

---

## Useful Tips

- **Process permits in January** — the city typically conducts business permit renewal drives at the start of the year.
- Bring extra copies of all documents.
- Ask the Negosyo Center about current processing times and requirements, which may change yearly.
