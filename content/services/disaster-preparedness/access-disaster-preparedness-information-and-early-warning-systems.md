# Cabuyao City: Disaster Preparedness Information and Early Warning Systems

The **Cabuyao City DRRMO (CDRRMO)** maintains a 24/7 operations center and multiple early warning systems to alert residents before and during disasters.

---

## Early Warning Systems in Cabuyao

| System                             | What It Does                                   | Location / Scope                           |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| USHER Earthquake Monitoring Device | Detects seismic activity; triggers alerts      | Installed at Cabuyao City Hall             |
| Flood Early Warning Systems        | Monitors water levels; triggers flood warnings | Marinig area and high-risk lakeshore zones |
| Sirens / Alarms                    | Audible emergency alerts                       | City and barangay level                    |
| SMS / Hotline Alerts               | Direct warnings via text                       | CDRRMO-managed network                     |
| Barangay Announcements             | Local PA system and community announcements    | All 18 barangays                           |
| Social Media Advisories            | Facebook updates from CDRRMO and LGU           | City-wide and real-time                    |

---

## How to Stay Informed

**During normal times (preparedness):**

- Follow the **Cabuyao City LGU** and **CDRRMO** official Facebook pages
- Know your **Barangay Hall's** contact number and Facebook page
- Know where your nearest evacuation center is (see Disaster Drills guide)

**During typhoon season or elevated risk:**

- Monitor **PAGASA** weather advisories (pagasa.dost.gov.ph)
- Check **CDRRMO** social media for local updates
- Listen to barangay PA announcements
- Check **Signal Number levels** from PAGASA for typhoon intensity

**During an active disaster:**

- **CDRRMO Hotlines:** 0966-690-7561 / 0995-672-8959
- Barangay announcements (PA system)
- Stay off roads unless evacuating

## City DRRM Plan and Structure

Cabuyao maintains a fully approved **Local DRRM Plan (LDRRMP)** with contingency plans for:

- Flooding
- Earthquakes
- Health emergencies (pandemic-ready protocols)

The **City DRRM Council (CDRRMC)** is chaired by the Mayor and includes the Police, Fire, Health, Engineering offices, and all barangay representatives. It holds regular quarterly planning meetings.

**Budget:** At least **5% of the city budget** is allocated to the DRRM Fund, covering equipment, training, relief goods, and infrastructure.

---

## CDRRMO Operations Center

The CDRRMO maintains a **24/7 operations center** that:

- Monitors weather, flood, and seismic data in real time
- Coordinates with barangays, police, fire, and hospitals during emergencies
- Manages the **Incident Command System (ICS)** during active disasters
- Tracks evacuation center registrations
