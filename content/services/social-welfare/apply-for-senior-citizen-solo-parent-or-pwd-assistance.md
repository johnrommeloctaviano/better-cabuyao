# Cabuyao City: Assistance for Senior Citizens, Solo Parents, and PWDs

The **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)** of Cabuyao City provides dedicated assistance programs for senior citizens, solo parents, and persons with disabilities (PWDs). These programs are available to all qualified Cabuyao residents.

---

## Senior Citizen Welfare

### Who Qualifies

- 60 years old and above
- Bona fide resident of Cabuyao City

### Available Benefits

| Benefit                           | Source          | Notes                                                     |
| --------------------------------- | --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Social Pension                    | DSWD (national) | Priority: no pension income, frail/sick, no stable income |
| Cash assistance                   | LGU Cabuyao     | Emergency and supplemental aid                            |
| Medical and burial support        | CSWDO / AICS    | For indigent senior citizens                              |
| Senior Citizen ID processing      | OSCA / Barangay | Required to access discounts and benefits                 |
| Community activities and programs | CSWDO           | Health, social, and recreational programs                 |

### How to Apply

1. Go to your **Barangay Hall** to register as a senior citizen and get a Certificate of Residency.
2. Process your **Senior Citizen ID** through the **Office for Senior Citizens Affairs (OSCA)** or barangay.
3. For financial or medical assistance, proceed to the **CSWDO Office** (see process below).

---

## Solo Parent Welfare

### Who Qualifies

Must prove single-parent status due to:

- Death of spouse
- Legal separation or annulment
- Abandonment by spouse

### Available Benefits

| Benefit                             | Details                                            |
| ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Solo Parent ID                      | Required to access priority programs and discounts |
| Financial and livelihood assistance | Cash aid and livelihood program priority           |
| Counseling and legal referrals      | Family and legal support services                  |
| Priority access to LGU programs     | Scholarships, housing, livelihood                  |

### Requirements

- Affidavit or legal proof of solo parent status
- PSA Birth Certificate of child/children
- Valid ID
- Barangay Certificate of Residency

### How to Apply

1. Go to your **Barangay Hall** for a Certificate of Indigency and referral.
2. Bring complete documents to the **CSWDO Office, Cabuyao City Hall**.
3. Undergo assessment by a social worker.

## Persons with Disabilities (PWD) Services

### Who Qualifies

- Any resident with a medical certification of disability (physical, sensory, intellectual, or psychosocial)

### Available Benefits

| Benefit                   | Details                                                           |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| PWD ID                    | Access to 20% discount on medicine, transport, food, and services |
| Financial assistance      | Emergency and supplemental cash aid                               |
| Assistive devices         | Wheelchairs and other equipment (subject to availability)         |
| Livelihood support        | Priority in livelihood programs and starter kits                  |
| Social inclusion programs | Community activities and awareness programs                       |

### Requirements

- Medical abstract or certificate of disability (from a licensed physician)
- Barangay Certificate of Residency
- Valid ID of applicant and/or guardian

### How to Apply

1. Get your **medical certificate** from a licensed physician confirming your disability.
2. Go to your **Barangay Hall** for a referral and Certificate of Residency.
3. Submit requirements to the **CSWDO** or **PWD Affairs Office, Cabuyao City Hall**.

---

## General Process for Any CSWDO Assistance

1. **Go to your Barangay Hall first** — get a Certificate of Indigency and referral letter. Barangay endorsement significantly improves approval chances.
2. **Prepare all documents before your visit** — missing documents are the most common cause of delays or rejection.
3. **Go early to the CSWDO** — arrive before 8:00 AM; avoid Mondays and days following major disasters (queues are longest).
4. **Social worker interview** — be clear, honest, and direct about your situation and needs.
5. **Wait for approval** — urgent cases may be resolved the same day; others take a few days.
6. **Follow up** — return on the advised date with your claim stub.
