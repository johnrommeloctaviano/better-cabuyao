# Cabuyao City: Maternal Care and Child Immunization

The City Health Offices (CHOs) and Barangay Health Centers (BHCs) of Cabuyao City provide **free prenatal and postnatal care**, **nutrition monitoring**, and **routine child immunization** for all residents.

---

## Maternal Care Services

| Service                                                          | Where                          | Schedule                                    | Cost     |
| ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Prenatal check-ups (midwife/nurse; physician referral as needed) | Barangay Health Centers, CHO I | Mon–Thu, 8:00 AM–7:00 PM (varies by center) | **Free** |
| Postnatal care                                                   | Barangay Health Centers, CHO I | Mon–Thu, 8:00 AM–7:00 PM                    | **Free** |
| Operation Timbang (nutrition monitoring)                         | Barangay Health Centers        | Scheduled per barangay                      | **Free** |
| Tetanus Toxoid vaccine + supplements                             | Barangay Health Centers        | During prenatal visits                      | **Free** |
| Family planning counseling and contraceptives                    | Barangay Health Centers, CHO I | Mon–Thu, 8:00 AM–7:00 PM                    | **Free** |

**Tip:** Bring a valid ID. First-time patients will be registered and issued a prenatal record at the health center.

---

## Child Immunization (EPI)

Routine vaccines are provided free under the **Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI)**.

| Age / Visit                          | Vaccines                                           | Where                           | Requirements                              | Cost     |
| ------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------- |
| Birth – 12 months (per DOH schedule) | BCG, Pentavalent, OPV/IPV, MMR, Hepatitis B, PCV13 | Barangay Health Centers / CHO I | ECCD card (issued onsite for first visit) | **Free** |
| Subsequent doses per DOH schedule    | Per EPI protocol                                   | Barangay Health Centers         | ECCD card                                 | **Free** |

> **First-timers:** Health workers will issue an ECCD card and give you a return schedule for all subsequent doses.

---

## Nutrition Programs

- **Operation Timbang** — city-wide weighing to detect and address undernutrition in children under 5
- **Supplementary feeding** — for malnourished and at-risk children, coordinated with Barangay Nutrition Scholars (BNS)
- **Mother's classes** — health education on breastfeeding, nutrition, and child development

---

## Reproductive Health & Family Planning

Available at CHO I and all Barangay Health Centers:

- Contraceptives: pills, injectables, condoms (free under DOH programs)
- Family planning counseling
- Teen reproductive health education

---

## How to Avail

1. **Go to your Barangay Health Center** during clinic hours.
2. **Register** — staff will issue or update your prenatal record or child's ECCD card.
3. **Attend scheduled visits** as advised by your midwife or nurse.
4. **For vaccines**, note the immunization day for your barangay and return on schedule.
