# Cabuyao City: Mental Health Services — YAKAP Program

**City Health Office II (CHO II)** provides free mental health counseling and psychosocial support through the **YAKAP Program**, a **PhilHealth-accredited** service available to all Cabuyao City residents.

---

## What is the YAKAP Program?

YAKAP is Cabuyao City's mental health program operated through CHO II. It provides professional mental health care at **no out-of-pocket cost** for eligible residents, covered by PhilHealth under Universal Health Care.

> PhilHealth accreditation for YAKAP is valid through **December 2026**.

---

## Services Available

| Service                 | Details                                               |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Individual counseling   | One-on-one sessions with a mental health professional |
| Psychosocial support    | Group and community-level emotional support           |
| Crisis intervention     | Immediate assistance for mental health emergencies    |
| PhilHealth-covered care | Services billed to PhilHealth for eligible members    |

## How to Avail

1. **Visit CHO II** during operating hours.
2. **Tell the staff** you are seeking mental health support under the YAKAP program.
3. **Register** and you will be scheduled for an initial assessment.
4. **Bring your PhilHealth ID** (if available) to avail of covered sessions.

> You do not need a referral to access YAKAP. Anyone may walk in.

---

## Who Can Avail

All Cabuyao City residents experiencing:

- Stress, anxiety, or depression
- Trauma or grief
- Crisis situations
- Substance use concerns
- Other mental health or emotional challenges
