# Cabuyao City: Public Facilities — Gyms, Barangay Halls, and Multipurpose Centers

The City Government of Cabuyao maintains public community facilities across all 18 barangays, from covered courts and multipurpose halls to evacuation centers and government buildings.

---

## Types of Public Facilities

| Facility                | Location                | Common Uses                                                         |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Barangay Halls          | All 18 barangays        | Barangay governance, community meetings, relief distribution        |
| Covered Courts          | Most barangays          | Sports, community events, livelihood training, program distribution |
| Multipurpose Halls      | Selected barangays      | Assemblies, seminars, feeding programs                              |
| Daycare / CDC Buildings | All 18 barangays        | Early childhood education (ECCD)                                    |
| Health Centers          | All barangays           | Primary health care (see Health Services guide)                     |
| Evacuation Centers      | Designated per barangay | Emergency shelter during typhoons, floods, fires                    |
| City Hall               | Sala, Cabuyao City      | All city government services                                        |
| Cabuyao City Hospital   | Cabuyao City            | Referral hospital (see Health Services guide)                       |

---

## How to Use a Covered Court or Multipurpose Hall

1. **Go to your Barangay Hall** and speak with the Barangay Captain or Secretary.
2. **Submit a request / reservation** with:
   - Purpose of use
   - Date and time
   - Name and contact number of requesting party
3. **Barangay approval** — the Barangay Captain or Council approves based on availability and purpose.
4. **Coordinate logistics** — some facilities require a minimal fee for utilities or cleaning.

> Community, civic, and government-related activities are generally prioritized.

---

## City-Level Facilities

For use of city-level halls and facilities (e.g., for permits, events, or programs):

- **City Hall** — Sala, Cabuyao City: all permits, business, and government services
- **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)**
- **City Youth & Sports Development Office (CYSDO)** — for sports facilities and events

Contact the relevant office directly to inquire about availability and reservation procedures.

---

## Evacuation Centers

During disasters (typhoons, flooding, fires), designated evacuation centers are activated by the **City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO)** in coordination with barangays.

- Your **Barangay Hall** will announce the nearest evacuation center.
- Bring essential documents, medicines, and clothing.
- Relief operations are coordinated by CSWDO and CDRRMO.
