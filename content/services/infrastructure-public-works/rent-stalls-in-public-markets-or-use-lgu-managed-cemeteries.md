# Cabuyao City: Public Markets and Cemetery Services

The City Government of Cabuyao manages public market facilities and a public cemetery, both of which offer stall rental and usage services to residents.

---

## Public Market Stalls

### How to Apply for a Market Stall

1. **Visit Cabuyao City Hall** — go to the **Market Administration Office** to inquire about available stalls and current vacancies.
2. **Submit requirements** (typical):
   - Accomplished application form (available at the office)
   - Valid ID
   - Barangay Certificate of Residency
   - Proof of business (or simultaneous business permit application)
   - 2x2 ID photos
3. **Stall assignment** — based on availability; a queuing or lottery system may apply during high-demand periods.
4. **Sign the lease agreement** and pay the initial fees.
5. **Process your business permit** at the **Business Permits and Licensing Office (BPLO)** if not yet done.

### Stall Rental Obligations

- Monthly rental fees are paid at the **City Treasurer's Office**
- Maintain cleanliness of your stall area
- Comply with market operating hours and health standards (sanitary permit required)
- Renew your lease and business permit annually

### Fees

Rates vary by stall size, section, and type of goods sold. Confirm current rates at the **Market Administration Office**.

## LGU-Managed Cemetery

### Services Offered

| Service                        | Details                                           |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Lot rental / assignment        | For burial of deceased residents                  |
| Niche rentals                  | For above-ground interment                        |
| Transfer of remains            | Coordination for bone transfer after lease expiry |
| Death certificate coordination | Coordinated with the Local Civil Registry         |

### How to Apply for Cemetery Services

1. **Bring the death certificate** and valid ID of the requesting family member to the **Cemetery Administration** or **City Civil Registry Office**.
2. **Choose a lot or niche** based on current availability.
3. **Pay the applicable fees** at the **City Treasurer's Office**.
4. **Coordinate the burial schedule** with the cemetery administrator.

### Important Notes

- **Priority for indigent families:** Indigent residents may apply for reduced-fee or free burial assistance through the **CSWDO** (see Social Welfare — Get Family and Community Welfare Support).
- Rental periods apply to cemetery lots — confirm renewal requirements to avoid forfeiture.

## Key Contacts

- **Market Administration Office** — Cabuyao City Hall (stall rentals and market concerns)
- **City Civil Registry / Cemetery Administration** — death certificates, burial coordination
- **City Treasurer's Office** — rental and fee payments
- **BPLO** — business permit for market vendors
- **CSWDO** — burial assistance for indigent families
