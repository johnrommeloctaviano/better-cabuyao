# Cabuyao City: Report Damaged Roads, Bridges, or Drainage

The **City Engineering Office (CEO)** handles local infrastructure concerns — road repair, drainage, canal declogging, and flood mitigation. For national roads and highways, concerns are referred to the **Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH)**.

---

## Who Handles What

| Infrastructure                                     | Responsible Office             |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Barangay roads, concreting, pothole repair         | City Engineering Office (CEO)  |
| Drainage canals, flood mitigation (local)          | City Engineering Office (CEO)  |
| Street lighting (local roads)                      | City Engineering Office (CEO)  |
| National roads (Manila South Road, SLEX, Highways) | DPWH Laguna 2nd / 3rd District |
| Major bridges and flood control structures         | DPWH Laguna                    |
| Expressway infrastructure (SLEX, LLRNP)            | TollCo / DPWH                  |

---

## What You Can Request or Report to the City Engineering Office

**Roads**

- Road concreting (unpaved barangay roads)
- Asphalt overlay (deteriorated paved roads)
- Pothole repair
- Road widening (based on program budget)

**Drainage & Flooding**

- Canal cleaning and declogging
- Canal construction or expansion
- Pumping station activation (for flood-prone areas)
- Flood mitigation works

**Traffic & Safety**

- Road signage installation
- Speed bumps
- Pedestrian lane marking

**Structures**

- Barangay hall repair or construction
- Covered courts
- Daycare buildings
- Evacuation centers

**Street Lighting**

- LED streetlight installation
- Solar light requests for remote or dark areas

## Flood-Prone Areas (Priority)

These barangays have active flood control concerns and receive priority for drainage projects:

| Barangay    | Issue                              |
| ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Banay-Banay | Flooding near lower-lying areas    |
| Marinig     | Lakeshore flooding (Laguna de Bay) |
| Diezmo      | Drainage issues                    |
| Niugan      | Near Laguna Lake, flood-vulnerable |

The **Laguna Lakeshore Road Network Project (LLRNP)** — a major national infrastructure project — also functions as a flood protection barrier along the Cabuyao lakeshore.

## How to Report or Request

**Option 1: Through Your Barangay**

1. Report to your **Barangay Captain** or **Barangay Hall**.
2. The barangay endorses the request to the **City Engineering Office**.
3. The CEO schedules assessment and repair based on urgency and budget.

**Option 2: Directly at the City Engineering Office**

1. Visit the **City Engineering Office, Cabuyao City Hall**.
2. File a formal request or complaint with details:
   - Location of the damage (barangay, street name, landmark)
   - Type of problem (pothole, collapsed drainage, broken light, etc.)
   - Your name and contact number
3. An engineer will assess and prioritize the repair.

**Option 3: For National Roads**

- Report to **DPWH Laguna 2nd District Engineering Office** for concerns along the Manila South Road, national highways, or bridges.
