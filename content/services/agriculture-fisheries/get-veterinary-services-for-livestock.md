# Cabuyao City: Veterinary Services for Livestock and Poultry

The **City Agriculture Office (CAO)** — in coordination with the **City Veterinarian's Office** — provides free veterinary services and livestock dispersal programs to registered farmers in Cabuyao City.

---

## Free Veterinary Services

| Service                     | Details                                                                  |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Vaccination                 | Animal disease prevention (Newcastle disease, hog cholera, rabies, etc.) |
| Deworming                   | For livestock and poultry                                                |
| Artificial insemination     | For cattle and swine improvement programs                                |
| Animal health consultations | Basic diagnosis and treatment guidance                                   |

These services are **free** for qualified residents. Bring your animals to the designated vaccination/deworming schedule, or coordinate with your **Barangay Agriculture Technician (BAT)** for on-site visits.

---

## Livestock Dispersal Programs

The city implements **dispersal programs** to provide starter animals to low-income farming households:

| Animal                   | Program Notes                                         |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Native chickens / layers | Dispersed to qualified indigent farming households    |
| Goats                    | Dispersed under DA / LGU programs                     |
| Swine                    | Select programs (confirm current availability at CAO) |

**Dispersal is a loan arrangement** — recipients are typically required to return the offspring (or equivalent animals) to the program for redistribution to the next beneficiary. Confirm current terms at the CAO.

---

## Who Can Apply

- Resident of Cabuyao City
- Has livestock / actively engaged in animal raising
- Registered in the **RSBSA** (for full program access)
- Priority: low-income / indigent farming households

---

## Active Barangays for Livestock Programs

Livestock programs are most active in farming and rural barangays:

**Bigaa, Butong, Gulod, Baclaran, Banay-Banay, Niugan, Sala, San Isidro, Pulo, Casile, Marinig, Mamatid**

---

## How to Access Veterinary Services

1. **Contact your Barangay Agriculture Technician (BAT)** — they can schedule on-site vaccination or deworming at your farm.
2. **Visit the City Agriculture Office (CAO)** at Cabuyao City Hall for scheduled vaccination days and livestock dispersal applications.
3. **Register in the RSBSA** if you haven't — required for dispersal programs.
4. **Join a farmers association** — organized groups get priority scheduling and access.
