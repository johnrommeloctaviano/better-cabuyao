# Cabuyao City: Relocation Sites and Displacement Assistance

If your household is affected by a **demolition, eviction, or relocation order** — particularly due to living in a danger zone or government-owned land — the **City Urban Development & Housing Affairs Office (CUDHAO)** is your first point of contact.

---

## Grounds for Relocation in Cabuyao

Residents may be subject to relocation under the following circumstances:

| Reason                                     | Examples                                                              |
| ------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Danger zone occupancy                      | Living along Laguna de Bay shoreline, riverbanks, or fault line areas |
| Government infrastructure projects         | Road widening, flood control works, public infrastructure             |
| Private land demolition (with court order) | Occupying privately owned land without legal right                    |
| Disaster damage (uninhabitable structures) | Post-flood or post-fire homes declared unsafe                         |

---

## Your Rights Before Demolition

Under **Republic Act 7279 (Urban Development and Housing Act)** and related laws, residents facing demolition have the right to:

- **Adequate notice** — at least 30 days written notice before demolition (for non-danger zones)
- **Consultation** — affected families must be consulted before relocation
- **Relocation assistance** — government is required to provide a relocation site or housing before demolition in most cases
- **Legal recourse** — you may seek assistance from the **Public Attorney's Office (PAO)** if you believe your rights are being violated

> **Exception:** Residents in **danger zones** (flood-prone, riverbanks, fault lines) may be subject to immediate or expedited relocation due to safety requirements.

---

## How to Find Out About Relocation Sites

1. **Go to CUDHAO** — City Urban Development & Housing Affairs Office, Cabuyao City Hall.
2. Ask about:
   - Current available relocation sites
   - Your household's eligibility (profiling status)
   - Applicable housing program (NHA, CMP, 4PH, socialized housing)
3. **Register as a beneficiary** if not yet profiled.
4. Submit required documents (valid ID, barangay certificate, proof of current residence, income documents).

## Relocation Priority Zones (2026)

These barangays have the highest number of households flagged for relocation:

| Barangay                              | Reason                                                 |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Bigaa, Butong, Gulod                  | Laguna de Bay lakeshore — flood and easement violation |
| Banlic, Mamatid (low-lying)           | Riverbank easements                                    |
| Sala (lower areas), Marinig, Baclaran | Moderate flood zone                                    |

> Approximately **723 households** were identified as being in danger zones as of the latest city assessment.

---

## Types of Relocation Housing Available

| Type                             | Description                                                                   |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Off-site resettlement            | Relocation to a designated housing area outside the original barangay         |
| In-city relocation               | Housing within Cabuyao City where land is available (preferred when feasible) |
| Mid-rise housing (4PH program)   | Vertical housing units under the national Pambansang Pabahay program          |
| Community Mortgage Program (CMP) | Collective land purchase for organized communities                            |

---

## Social Preparation for Relocation

CUDHAO and CSWDO provide **social preparation services** for relocating families:

- Pre-relocation counseling and orientation
- Livelihood assistance links (DOLE, DTI programs)
- CSWDO support for vulnerable households (PWDs, seniors, solo parents)

---

## Key Contacts

- **CUDHAO (City Urban Development & Housing Affairs Office)** — Cabuyao City Hall (all housing, relocation, and displacement concerns)
- **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)** — welfare support for displaced families
- **Public Attorney's Office (PAO)** — free legal assistance if your rights are at risk
- **DHSUD Region IV-A** — Department of Human Settlements and Urban Development (national housing programs)
- **National Housing Authority (NHA)** — government housing units and relocation sites
