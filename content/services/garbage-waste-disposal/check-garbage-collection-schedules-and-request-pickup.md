# Cabuyao City: Garbage Collection Schedules and Pickup Requests

The City Government of Cabuyao manages garbage collection through the **City Environment & Natural Resources Office (CENRO)** and **General Services Office (GSO)**, with collection operations carried out by a contracted hauling service.

> **Before 5:30 AM is the best time to put out your trash** — morning shift collection begins at 5:30 AM across most barangays.

---

## Collection Schedules by Area (2026)

Cabuyao uses a **segregated, rotating weekly schedule**. Put out the correct type of waste on the designated day.

| Waste Type                                       | Collection Frequency                  |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| Biodegradable (food waste, leaves)               | 2x per week                           |
| Non-biodegradable / Residual (plastics, sachets) | 1–2x per week                         |
| Recyclables                                      | Separate schedule or via barangay MRF |

### Poblacion (Barangay Uno, Dos, Tres) — City Center / Commercial

| Day                  | Collection Type                           |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Monday / Thursday    | Biodegradable                             |
| Tuesday / Friday     | Non-biodegradable                         |
| Wednesday / Saturday | Light collection / recyclables / overflow |

> Highest collection frequency — commercial and government center zone.

### Major Urban Barangays (Mamatid, Marinig, Niugan, Pulo)

| Day                  | Collection Type   |
| -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Monday / Thursday    | Biodegradable     |
| Wednesday / Saturday | Non-biodegradable |
| Sunday               | No collection     |

> These are Cabuyao's most densely populated barangays — trucks often make extra passes.

### Lakeshore Barangays (Baclaran, Butong, Bigaa, Gulod)

| Day                  | Collection Type   |
| -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Tuesday / Friday     | Biodegradable     |
| Wednesday / Saturday | Non-biodegradable |

> Schedule is offset to avoid waste buildup before heavy rains in flood-prone areas.

### Banay-Banay, Banlic, Sala, Diezmo

| Day                  | Collection Type   |
| -------------------- | ----------------- |
| Monday / Thursday    | Biodegradable     |
| Wednesday / Saturday | Non-biodegradable |

### Peripheral / Lower-Density Barangays (Casile, Pittland, San Isidro)

- 2–3 collection passes per week total
- Typically: 1 biodegradable day + 1 residual day + 1 optional pass
- Lower waste volume — fewer route passes

### National Highway / Commercial Corridors

- Daily or near-daily pickup for high-traffic commercial zones along the highway

> **Note:** Schedules may be adjusted by the LGU. Always confirm the current schedule with your **Barangay Hall** or check the **Cabuyao City LGU Facebook page** and **Barangay Facebook pages**.

## Collection Timing

| Shift                | Time               | Coverage                                         |
| -------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Morning (primary)    | 5:30 AM – 10:00 AM | Residential areas, barangay routes               |
| Midday (second pass) | 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM | Overflow areas, commercial zones, missed pickups |
| Night (limited)      | Evening            | High-traffic roads and highway clearing only     |

---

## How to Request Special or Bulk Pickup

For large items (furniture, appliances, construction debris):

1. **Go to your Barangay Hall** and request a special collection schedule.
2. The barangay coordinates with the **CENRO / GSO** for a separate hauling arrangement.
3. Construction waste and demolition debris may require separate disposal — confirm at CENRO.

> Collection trucks are compactor trucks, dump trucks, or mini dump trucks (for narrow barangay roads).
