# Cabuyao City: LGU Educational Support Programs

Beyond scholarships, the City Government of Cabuyao provides direct educational support to students, schools, and youth in the community.

---

## 1. Student Assistance

| Program                      | Details                                                                    | Who Benefits                               |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| School supplies distribution | Notebooks, pens, and basic materials distributed to public school students | Public elementary and high school students |
| Uniform assistance           | Uniform support for selected barangays                                     | Identified beneficiaries per barangay      |
| Feeding programs             | Nutritional feeding for daycare and elementary students                    | Daycare enrollees, malnourished students   |

---

## 2. Infrastructure Support

The LGU supports public school infrastructure in coordination with DepEd:

- **Classroom construction and repair** — ongoing through DepEd regional programs
- **ICT support and digitalization** — computer labs and digital learning materials (availability varies per year and school)

---

## 3. Youth Development Programs (CYSDO)

The **City Youth & Sports Development Office (CYSDO)** runs programs that complement formal education:

- **Leadership training** for youth and student leaders
- **Skills development workshops** (livelihood, entrepreneurship, civic engagement)
- **Sports and academic competitions** — from barangay-level to inter-city events

---

## 4. Special Education (SPED)

Cabuyao has access to special education facilities for learners with disabilities:

| Facility                                       | Services                                                                    |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| The Academy of Hope (and similar SPED schools) | Therapy-based learning, inclusive education support                         |
| DepEd SPED centers                             | Serving learners with visual, hearing, intellectual, and other disabilities |

For referrals, contact the **DepEd Schools Division of Cabuyao** or the **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)**.

---

## 5. Alternative Learning System (ALS)

Run by **DepEd**, ALS provides a pathway to education for:

- Out-of-school youth
- Working students who cannot attend regular school

ALS is equivalent to an elementary or high school diploma and follows a flexible schedule. Inquire at the **DepEd Schools Division of Cabuyao** or the nearest public school.

---

## Contacts

- **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)** — daycare, feeding, and welfare programs
- **City Youth & Sports Development Office (CYSDO)** — scholarships, youth programs
- **DepEd Schools Division of Cabuyao** — public school programs, ALS, SPED
