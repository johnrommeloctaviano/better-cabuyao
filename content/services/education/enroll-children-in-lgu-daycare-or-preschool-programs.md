# Cabuyao City: Enroll Children in LGU Daycare or Preschool Programs

Every barangay in Cabuyao City has at least one **Child Development Center (CDC)** — also called a Barangay Day Care Center or ECCD Center — managed by the **City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO)**. Enrollment is **free**.

---

## Who Can Enroll

- Children aged **3 to 4 years old**
- Residents of the corresponding barangay

---

## What Daycare Centers Offer

- Early childhood education aligned with the ECCD curriculum
- Feeding programs
- Basic health monitoring (weighing, developmental checks)
- Parenting seminars for guardians

---

## Barangay Daycare Centers — All 18 Barangays

Each of Cabuyao's 18 barangays has at least one CDC. Larger, more populated barangays have multiple centers.

| Area                     | Barangays with Daycare Centers                       |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Poblacion (City Center)  | Barangay Uno, Barangay Dos, Barangay Tres            |
| National Highway / Urban | Baclaran, Banay-Banay, Banlic, Bigaa, Butong, Diezmo |
| Laguna de Bay Side       | Gulod, Mamatid, Marinig, Niugan, Pulo, Sala          |

> **Mamatid, Marinig, and Pulo** have multiple daycare centers due to their large populations (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.).

---

## How to Find Your Nearest Daycare

Most daycare centers are **not listed online**. They are typically located:

- Inside the barangay hall
- Inside a covered court or community building
- In a barangay annex

**The most reliable method:** Go to your barangay hall and ask: _"Saan po ang daycare center dito?"_ — staff will direct you immediately.

---

## How to Enroll

1. **Visit your barangay hall** or daycare center.
2. **Submit the following documents:**
   - PSA Birth Certificate of the child
   - Barangay Certificate (residency)
   - Immunization record
3. **Wait for the enrollment schedule** — usually announced in **May to June**.
4. Classes typically begin in **June** following the school year calendar.

**Cost:** Free
