# Cabuyao City: Schools, Colleges, and Educational Institutions

Cabuyao City has a complete education pipeline — from barangay daycare through college — supported by a mix of LGU, national government, and private institutions.

---

## Public Schools (K–12) — Free

The **Department of Education (DepEd) Schools Division of Cabuyao** oversees all public schools.

| Level                     | Number of Schools |
| ------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Public Elementary Schools | 19                |
| Public High Schools       | 8                 |

**How to Enroll in a Public School**

1. Visit the **nearest public school** during enrollment period (DepEd schedule, typically June / Brigada Eskwela period).
2. Submit:
   - PSA Birth Certificate
   - Report card (for transferees)
   - Proof of address (barangay certificate or any document showing Cabuyao residence)
3. Follow the school's enrollment and section assignment process.

**Cost:** Free

## Private Schools (K–12 and Senior High School)

Cabuyao has **100+ private schools**, including:

| School                                         | Notes                            |
| ---------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| St. Matthew Montessori and Science High School | STEM and science-focused tracks  |
| Our Lady of Assumption College                 | Academic and vocational programs |
| Liceo de Cabuyao                               | Multi-level institution          |

- Many private SHS programs accept **DepEd SHS Vouchers**, which subsidize tuition.
- Private schools generally open enrollment **January–March**.

## Colleges and Universities

| Institution                     | Type                      | Known For                                                           |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pamantasan ng Cabuyao**       | LGU-run public university | IT, Engineering, Education, Nursing — most affordable for residents |
| Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna   | Private                   | Engineering and technology programs                                 |
| St. Vincent College of Cabuyao  | Private                   | Multi-disciplinary programs; offers financial aid                   |
| Cabuyao Institute of Technology | Private                   | Technical and vocational college                                    |

> **Pamantasan ng Cabuyao** is the most accessible option for Cabuyao residents — as the LGU university, it offers the lowest tuition and priority for city scholars.
