# Cabuyao City: Apply for Local Scholarships

Cabuyao City offers active scholarship programs for college students through the **City Youth & Sports Development Office (CYSDO)** and various partner institutions.

---

## 1. "Iskolar ng Bagong Cabuyao" — LGU Scholarship

The city's flagship scholarship for college-level residents.

**Benefits**

- Tuition support
- Monthly allowance (disbursed via ATM card)
- Possible school supplies assistance

**Who Can Apply**

- Bona fide resident of Cabuyao City
- Currently enrolled or incoming college student
- Good academic standing
- From a low-income / qualified household

**Requirements (typical)**

- Latest grades / transcript
- Certificate of residency (from barangay)
- Income documents (e.g., DSWD certificate, ITR, barangay indigency certificate)
- Valid ID

**How to Apply**

1. **Monitor the official announcement** — applications are announced yearly via the **Cabuyao City LGU Facebook page** and **CYSDO** channels (example intake: October).
2. **Submit the required documents** to the CYSDO office during the open application period.
3. **Undergo screening and interview** as part of the evaluation process.
4. Wait for results and onboarding instructions.

**Contact:** City Youth & Sports Development Office (CYSDO), Cabuyao City Hall

## 2. School-Based Scholarships

Several schools in Cabuyao offer their own financial aid:

| School                         | Scholarship Type                                         |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| St. Vincent College of Cabuyao | Financial aid for students with demonstrated need        |
| Pamantasan ng Cabuyao          | LGU university — lowest tuition option for residents     |
| Various private schools        | Academic, entrance exam discounts, athletic scholarships |

> Contact the Registrar or Scholarship Office of your target school for current offerings and requirements.

---

## 3. Senior High School (SHS) Voucher Program

Students enrolled in private Senior High Schools may qualify for **DepEd's SHS Voucher Program**, which subsidizes tuition. Apply through your school's registrar.

## Tips

- **Check the Cabuyao City LGU Facebook page regularly** — scholarship openings are announced there first.
- Apply early; slots are competitive and limited.
- Prepare all documents in advance (especially residency certificates and income proofs, which take time to process).
